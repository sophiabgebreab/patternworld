Our Attractions

Grounds: Patterns in Nature

Fractal Forest

Discover the beauty of nature's designs in the Fractal Forest, where each tree and leaf showcases the mathematical wonder of fractals.

Geometric Gardens

Wander through the Geometric Gardens, a peaceful, grid-based garden featuring meticulously arranged plants that play with stunning geometric patterns.

Rides

Motif Mountain

Embark on a thrilling journey through various cultural motifs and patterns. This roller coaster ride takes you through vibrant scenes inspired by global designs, providing an exhilarating experience with every twist and turn.

Pattern Playground

Designed for our younger visitors, the Pattern Playground offers a fun and safe environment. Kids can enjoy the spiral slide, climb through a maze of shapes, and discover patterns from around the world in interactive play areas.

Town

Kaleidoscope Castle

Step inside Kaleidoscope Castle, where every wall and window is a constantly shifting mosaic of colors and shapes. This magical experience is sure to captivate visitors of all ages as they explore the castle's many heavily-patterened rooms and corridors.

Tessellation Tower

At the heart of PatternWorld, you'll find the Tessellation Tower. Climb to the top of Tessellation Tower for a breathtaking view of PatternWorld. The tower itself is a marvel of design, covered in intricate tessellations that fit together perfectly. It's the perfect spot to take in the park's full splendor and witness the entire patchwork landscape of PatternWorld!

Pattern Path

To navigate the park, just follow the Pattern Path! Stroll along a walkway adorned with over one hundred unique patterns from around the globe. Keep an eye out for the Pattern Posts— each section of the path is marked with informational signs that explain the history and cultural significance of the patterns you encounter on your adventure!
